Frequently Asked Questions

Oplentia is a platform where entrepreneurs can showcase their startup ideas and connect with investors and collaborators. Our system helps evaluate ideas based on their potential, visibility, and engagement. Investors and stakeholders can explore promising startups and directly connect with founders for collaboration, mentorship, or funding opportunities.

Oplentia is open to student founders, early-stage entrepreneurs, innovators, and investors who are looking to discover or support promising startup ideas. Whether you have a new idea or want to invest in emerging ventures, Oplentia provides a platform to connect and grow together.

Oplentia follows a growth-driven model. Entrepreneurs can submit and showcase their ideas freely. Once a startup gains traction and connects with investors or stakeholders through the platform, Oplentia charges a small success-based platform fee. This helps us sustain the platform and continue supporting new innovative ideas.
